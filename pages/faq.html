❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What is FocusFlow?

FocusFlow is a joyful productivity companion that combines the Pomodoro technique, task management, and gamification to help you stay focused and motivated.

How does the timer work?

Go to the Focus Lab, set your work and break durations, and hit start. When a session ends, you'll get a notification and earn points.

Is my data safe?

All data is stored only in your browser's localStorage. No information is sent to a server.

Can I use it on my phone?

Absolutely! The layout adapts to small screens, and the sidebar collapses into a hamburger menu.
